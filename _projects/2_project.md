<h4 class="mt-3" id="household-applications"><strong>Household Applications</strong></h4>
<p>This is a project I worked on during my visit to the <a href="http://prg.cs.umd.edu/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Perception and Robotics Group</a> at the <a href="https://umd.edu/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">University of Maryland</a>, College Park, Sep-Dec 2016. A mobile manipulation robot was developed for housework in a kitchen scenario, where I mainly took charge of the sub-task of table cleaning.
